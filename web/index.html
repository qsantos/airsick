Advised Introduction to Rocket Science (Including Crashes) for Kerbals

A website/booklet covering the basics of spaceflight and orbital mechanics, inspired by Kerbal Space Program.

Browse the web version | Download the PDF

Table of contents

Introduction
A short introduction describing the document.
Basics
A few notations and concepts from mathematics and physics that can be useful to understand the formulas from next chapters.
Mechanics
How stuff moves, and how we try to efficiently predict its position at some point in the future.
Orbits
How orbits work, how they can be described with a few numbers, and how we can use them to predict a trajectory.
Launch
How a spacecraft can lift from the ground all the way up to its first stable orbit.
Maneuvers
How a spacecraft can change its trajectory, and how much such a maneuver would cost.
Operating
Some practical matters to take into considerations when planing missions for orbiters or landers.
